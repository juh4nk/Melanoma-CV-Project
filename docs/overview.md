## Core Problem
- The timing of when melanoma is caught is critical to the success rates of treatment
- However, there are 2 fundamental barriers for most individuals:
    - going to the doctor for every spot is infeasible
    - identifying and monitoring spots requires specialized training and practice
- This project seeks to bridge the gap between at-risk individuals and receiving early warnings about potential malignant spots

## Key Features
- Functionality to mimc the ABCDE method used by medical professionals to identify melanoma
- A medium to feasibly put this tool in the hands of those with concerns about spots
- A feedback mechanism to gather user experience and evaluate effectiveness of tooling provided

## Chosen Tooling
- To mimic the ABCDE method, machine learning will be utilized through Python libaries such as TensorFlow
- To put this tool in the right hands, an Android application will be developed to encapsulate the ML tool
- To gather feedback, optional internal and external data collection methods will be offered to users, such as surveys
